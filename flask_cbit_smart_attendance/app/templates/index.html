SmAtt - Smart Attendance

SmAtt is a platform that enables teachers to automate the attendance process.

SmAtt is more than just automated attendance.

SmAtt enables proxy-free attendance using 2 level verification.

Try the live demo







Using SmAtt is easy!

SmAtt not only automates the attendance process but is also very easy to use. There are just 4 steps.

Step 1

Visit the website.

Step 2

Capture the photograph of your ID Card.

Step 3

Capture your face in the face recognition software.

Step 4

Attendance has been captured.

The admin login feature prevents access to the website without authentication.
